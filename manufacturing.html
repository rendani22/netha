MANUFACTURING

HYDRO CYCLONE

Netha Engineering Technology manufactures a range of cyclones for classification and dense medium separation. We manufacture cyclones with sizes ranging from 15mm – 1500mm.

Steel Ceramic Lined Cyclones

Netha cyclones feature a unique 270-degree scroll, designed to provide better feed distribution of material inside the cyclone compared to traditional designs. Our cyclones can be lined with 92% to 95% alumina tile with different thicknesses ranging from 6mm to 50mm.

Cast Iron Cyclones

The Netha cast iron cyclones are of 27% high chrome for applications with excessively high impact. Available sizes range from 250mm – 1000mm diameter.

Polyurethane Cyclones

Netha polyurethane cyclones are used in de-slimming, dewatering, backfilling, tailings dams and milling applications. The available sizes range from 100mm to 350mm diameter.
