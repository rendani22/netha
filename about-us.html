Who we are

Netha Engineering Technology is a proprietary limited company and our head office is at number 156 Kuscke Street in Meadowdale, Johannesburg in the Republic of South Africa. The company offers off- and on-site engineering services to the minerals processing plants generally within the Southern African mining industry.

MISSION

We provide high quality engineering solutions and services at a highly competitive price within the shortest lead times possible. Our motto is to get it right the first time. It is our company’s goal to have 100% customer satisfaction with respect to quality, professionalism and speed to market. We also constantly strive to discover new ways to exceed customers’ expectations while doing so at the lowest possible cost

VISION

Our vision is to become a leading supplier of high quality products and engineering solutions to the mineral processing industry, within the shortest turnaround time and most cost effective price. Through our dedicated people, we endeavour to provide an unparalleled aftermarket support to our clientele, backed by stringent in-house quality control systems.

VALUES

At Netha Engineering Technology we strive to achieve and inherently live the following non-negotiable values:

  • Integrity and Trustworthiness: We earn our trust by doing what we say we will do
  • Respect: As an inherent part of our business, we respect our clients and our employees.
  • Accountability: We take ownership for our own actions.
  • Quality: We do it right the first time.
  • Innovations: We use innovative technologies and ideas to stay ahead of our competitors.

About Us

Remember The Past

Netha Engineering Technology has 10 years’ experience in process engineering, manufacturing and process equipment supply.

We have extensive metallurgical experience in gold, iron ore and diamond. Additionally, we have also worked in the mining industry as a Metallurgist, equipment supplier in manufacturing conducting sales and later moving up the ranks to take on leadership roles within the same sectors.

Going forward, Netha Engineering Technology envisions having additional leaders in Sales and Marketing, Human Resources, Information Systems and Operations.
