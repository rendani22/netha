    Netha Engineering Technology manufactures a range of cyclones for classification and dense medium separation. Hydro-cyclones

Screen Panels

We supply a comprehensive range of high quality injection molded polyurethane screen panels. Our polyurethane panel sizes vary from 0.2mm slot up to 200 mm square apertures in various thickness and surface features, including the standard V-R Flexi configuration, which are also available in a variety of sizes from 2mm.

pipes

We have a wide range of pipes, HDPE and steel. Our steel pipes have been designed for corrosive and erosive slurries. They are designed, built and lined with a combination of materials such as mild steel, 27% chrome, ceramic, rubber, grades of stainless steel and alloys as well as our newly developed in-house material.​

Transfer Chutes

Netha Engineering Technology designs, manufactures and installs transfer chutes of any size for all industries. The chutes are either polyurethane- which are prone to high impact and abrasion, or ceramic lined, depending on material chemical and physical properties, as well as the degree of abrasion and impact. ​
