MANUFACTURING

HYDRO CYCLONE

Netha Engineering Technology manufactures a range of cyclones for classification and dense medium separation. We manufacture cyclones with sizes ranging from 15mm – 1500mm.

Steel Ceramic Lined Cyclones

Netha cyclones feature a unique 270-degree scroll, designed to provide better feed distribution of material inside the cyclone compared to traditional designs. Our cyclones can be lined with 92% to 95% alumina tile with different thicknesses ranging from 6mm to 50mm.

Cast Iron Cyclones

The Netha cast iron cyclones are of 27% high chrome for applications with excessively high impact. Available sizes range from 250mm – 1000mm diameter.

Polyurethane Cyclones

Netha polyurethane cyclones are used in de-slimming, dewatering, backfilling, tailings dams and milling applications. The available sizes range from 100mm to 350mm diameter.

TRANSFER CHUTE

Netha Engineering Technology designs, manufactures and installs transfer chutes of any size for all industries. The chutes are either polyurethane- which are prone to high impact and abrasion, or ceramic lined, depending on material chemical and physical properties, as well as the degree of abrasion and impact.

INTER-STAGE SCREENS

Netha manufactures a range of inter-stage screens up to 1500 mm diameter using 316 stainless steel wire.

CONVEYOR IDLERS

Netha manufactures a range of HDPE, Polyurethane and Steel conveyor idlers ranging from 340 x 127 x 25mm to 1298 x 127 x 25mm.

SCREEN PANELS

We supply a comprehensive range of high quality injection molded polyurethane screen panels. Our polyurethane panel sizes varies from 0.2mm slot up to 200 mm square apertures in various thickness and surface features, including the standard V-R Flexi configuration, which are also available in a variety of sizes from 2mm.​

SPRAY NOZZLES

Our standard polyurethane spray nozzles are characterized by resistance to abrasion and corrosion; and they have a low aptitude to clogging. We also supply self-cleaning spray nozzles for dirty water applications, to minimize downtime whilst maintaining screening efficiency and high utilization levels.​

HDPE PIPES

We have a wide range of pipes, HDPE and steel. Our steel pipes have been designed for corrosive and erosive slurries. They are designed, built and lined with a combination of materials such as mild steel, ceramic, rubber, grades of stainless steel and alloys as well as our newly developed in-house material.​

POLYURETHANE LINERS

Netha Engineering Technology manufactures a range of polyurethane liners suitable for applications that are prone to high impact and abrasion. The liners are manufactured in various shore hardness and thicknesses, depending on the degree of impact and abrasion of the material that is being processed.
