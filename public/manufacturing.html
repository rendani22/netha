MANUFACTURING

Learn more about our range of products that Netha Engineering Technology offers to the market.

HYDRO-CYCLONE

Netha Engineering Technology manufactures a range of cyclones for classification and dense medium separation. We manufacture cyclones with sizes ranging from 250 – 1500mm.

Steel Ceramic Lined Cyclones

Netha cyclones feature a unique 270-degree scroll, designed to provide better feed distribution of material inside the cyclone compared to traditional designs. Our cyclones can be lined with 92 - 95% alumina tile with different thicknesses ranging from 6 - 50mm.

Cast Iron Cyclones

Netha Engineering Cast Iron Cyclones are manufactured using 27% high Chrome for applications with excessively high impact. Available sizes range from 250 – 1000mm diameter cyclone.

Polyurethane Cyclones

Netha polyurethane cyclones are used for de-slimming, dewatering, backfilling, tailings dams and milling applications. The available sizes range from 100 - 350mm diameter.

TRANSFER CHUTE

Netha Engineering Technology designs, manufactures and installs transfer chutes of any size for all industries. Netha has different material of construction for lining the chutes, depending on the application and physical properties of the material.

INTER-STAGE SCREENS

Netha manufactures a range of inter-stage screens up to 1500 mm diameter using 316 stainless steel wire.

CONVEYOR IDLERS

Netha manufactures a range of HDPE, Polyurethane and Steel conveyor idlers ranging from 340 x 127 x 25mm to 1298 x 127 x 25mm.

SCREENING MEDIA

Netha supplies a comprehensive range of high quality injection molded polyurethane screen panels. Our polyurethane panel sizes varies from 0.2mm slot up to 200mm square apertures in various thickness and surface features, including the standard V-R Flexi configuration, which are also available in a variety of sizes from 2mm.​ Netha manufactures custom made mesh wire in various shapes and sizes depending on the application.

SPRAY NOZZLES

Our standard polyurethane spray nozzles are characterized by resistance to abrasion and corrosion; and they have a low aptitude to clogging. We also supply self-cleaning spray nozzles for dirty water applications, to minimize downtime whilst maintaining screening efficiency and high utilization levels.​

HDPE PIPES

We have a wide range of pipes, HDPE and steel. Our pipes have been designed for corrosive and erosive slurry application. We have unlimitted capability in manufacturing and design of steel and HDPE pipes.

POLYURETHANE LINERS

Netha Engineering Technology manufactures a range of polyurethane liners suitable for applications that are prone to high impact and abrasion. The liners are manufactured in various shore hardness and thicknesses, depending on the degree of impact and abrasion of the material that is being processed.
